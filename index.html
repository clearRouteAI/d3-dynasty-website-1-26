Emergency Response

Corridor Clearing

Ambulance publishes priority constraint. Every autonomous vehicle yields automatically. Path clears before arrival.

Mass Evacuation

City publishes evacuation constraints. All autonomous vehicles comply automatically. No counter-flow traffic.

Threat Perimeter

Law enforcement publishes no-entry zone. All autonomous vehicles reroute immediately.

Fire Scene Exclusion

Fire command establishes exclusion zone around active scene. Autonomous vehicles cannot enter operational area.

Protected Facilities

Nuclear plant restricts autonomous vehicles from approaching within 1 mile. All robotaxis automatically avoid the protected area.

Checkpoint Compliance

Border patrol requires vehicles to stop at inspection point. Autonomous vehicles cannot bypass checkpoints.
